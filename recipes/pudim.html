Pudim

Pudding aka Flan

Recipe by Livia Loiola

Description

Medium difficulty recipe, known for its creamy texture.

Prep time: 60 minutes. Makes 8 slices (portions).

Ingredients

Cream
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of milk (use condensed milk can for measure)
  • 3 eggs
Syrup
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

Cream
  1. Blend eggs well
  2. Add condensed milk and blend everything together
Syrup
  1. Melt sugar in a pan until brown, add water and let it thicken
  2. Pour syrup into the bottom and suirl it onto the sides of a Flan Mold adding the cream next.
  3. Bake it in the oven for 45 minutes at 350Â°F, with the round pan inside of a deeper 10.5x12.8 baking pan with water
  4. Gently stick a fork through the baked cream to check its consistency. If the fork comes back clean, the Pudim is done baking.
  5. Let it sit until cool and flip it into a round dish for storing in the fridge.
