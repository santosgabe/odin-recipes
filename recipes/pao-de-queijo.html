Brazilian Cheese Bread

Recipe by Elisa Bauer

Description

An easy to make recipe that will give you a little taste of Brazil.

Easy to achieve with widely available ingredients and little preparation time.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups (170 grams) tapioca flour
  • 1 1/2 cups grated cheese (your preference)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400째F. Spread a small amount olive oil around the insides of each well of a mini-muffin tin.
  2. Pulse all ingredients in a blender until smooth. Use spatula to mix ingredients stuck on blender walls.
  3. Pour batter into prepared mini-muffin tin; leave about 1/8 inch from the top.
  4. Bake at 400째F for 15-20 minutes, until puffy and nicely browned.
